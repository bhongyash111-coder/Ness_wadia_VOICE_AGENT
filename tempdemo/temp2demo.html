Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reach MESCOE campus?
From Pune Junction Railway Station: 1.2 km (~10 min walk). PMT Bus Stop: Bund Garden. Near Ruby Hall Clinic. Address: 19, Late Prin. V.K. Joag Path, Wadia College Campus, Pune ‚Äì 411001.
What UG courses are offered?
Computer Engineering (240), E&TC Engineering (120), Mechanical Engineering (120), and Automation & Robotics Engineering (60). All are 4-year BE programs affiliated to SPPU.
Is hostel facility available?
Yes. Separate Boys and Girls hostels are available within campus. Admission is on First Come First Served basis. Students must return by 10:00 PM. Visit: mespune.org/hostel/
How to apply for admission?
Call 7798883400 / 77988 84177. Fill enquiry form at mescoe.mespune.org/admission-registration. FE admissions are via MHT-CET (Centralized CAP). DTE Maharashtra: cetcell.mahacet.org
How to login to the ERP portal?
Login at portal.vmedulife.com/public/auth/#/login/mescoe-pune using your Student ID credentials. For access issues, contact the college office at (020)-26163831.
Where can I find previous question papers?
Previous year question papers for all branches are available at: mescoe.mespune.org/engineering-questions-papers. Login to access them for your department.
How do I apply for MahaDBT scholarship?
Fill the Google Form at forms.gle/pcHV6G6rNmTDvwEi6 and upload required documents. Check the scholarship notice dated October 2025 for eligibility criteria and deadlines.
Who do I contact for exam queries?
Email: exam.query@mescoepune.org. College Exam Officer: Dr. P. B. Chopade. Use the e-Grievance portal for formal complaints: portal.vmedulife.com/institute/e-grievance/home/mescoe-pune
